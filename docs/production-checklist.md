# Production Checklist

Before publishing or ordering samples:

- [ ] Confirm final front variant: neon or cream-only.
- [ ] Confirm garment color: Pepper first, Black backup.
- [ ] Check Printify mockup at realistic scale, not just close-up preview.
- [ ] Verify the chest hit is about 3.5 inches wide.
- [ ] Verify the back badge is about 11 to 12 inches wide.
- [ ] Order one physical sample before public launch.
- [ ] Photograph sample in the actual bar lighting.
- [ ] Publish only after the mockup and sample both feel retail-worthy.

## Operator note

Do not overbuild the capsule before the hero tee is good. One strong tee in the room will sell better than five average SKUs online.
